# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in the VibeBus community to be welcoming, professional, and constructive for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or platform.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and open to good-faith feedback.
- Focus discussion on behavior, evidence, and the software.
- Protect others' privacy and remove credentials, recovery material, local databases, and personal data from public discussion.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and help improve documentation and tests.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, doxxing, deliberate disclosure of private material, or disruption intended to prevent others from participating is not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and may restrict participation when this policy is violated. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's maintainer contact or GitHub reporting tools. Security issues must follow `SECURITY.md`, not a public report.
